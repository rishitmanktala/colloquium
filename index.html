01

Diplomacy Begins With Dialogue

Education is no longer confined to the classroom. At Suncity School, Colloquium MUN offers young minds a platform to explore diplomacy, develop empathy, and experience the power of dialogue in addressing complex global issues.

Delegates are invited to think like diplomats, engage like policymakers, and speak like leaders across committee rooms designed for research, strategy, and collaboration.

03

Committees And Agendas

Choose your room: crisis, climate, politics, press, law, finance, culture, or sport.

UNSC

Reviewing the rise of communal terrorism with special emphasis on the Sahel Region.

UNFCCC

Deliberation on reducing the effects of carbon emissions with special emphasis on sustainable energy sources.

GFMC

Deliberation on the Global Financial Crisis and measures to restore economic stability.

Founders Circle

Deliberating the ethical responsibility of tech founders in the age of AI — should billionaire entrepreneurs be regulated by democratic institutions?

IMI

Discussing Whether Influencer Culture Is Inspiring a Generation or Destroying It.

AIPPM

Analysing the Centralisation of Power and Federal Tensions Between State Governments and the Union Government.

CBI

The Head of the State has gone missing 24 hours before national elections. Intelligence failure, internal betrayal, or foreign conspiracy?

Mock Trial

US format (prosecution v/s defense). Case file will be revealed prior to the conference.

IPL Auction

Minimum: 2 members. Maximum: 4 members.

International Press

Inviting journalists, photographers, and caricaturists.
